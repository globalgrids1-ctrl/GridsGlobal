## The Critical Role of Miscellaneous Steel in Modern Construction

Miscellaneous steel, often referred to as “secondary steel,” includes components that are smaller in scale but critical in purpose. These elements frequently:

- Interface directly with architectural finishes  
- Connect to concrete, masonry, or structural steel  
- Support human occupancy and dynamic loading  
- Require strict compliance with building and safety codes  

Improper detailing of these elements can result in:

- Field rework and costly RFIs  
- Fabrication delays  
- Installation conflicts  
- Budget overruns  
- Safety risks  

Precision detailing at the modeling stage eliminates these downstream risks.
